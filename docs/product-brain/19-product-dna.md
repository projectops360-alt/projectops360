# 19 — Product DNA

The **immutable principles** of ProjectOps360°. These are not ADRs (decisions can be
superseded); these are **permanent**. Any work that violates a DNA principle is wrong by
definition. Changing this file requires founder-level intent and a recorded rationale.

---

1. **ProjectOps360° is not a task manager.** It is an AI-first Project Execution Operating
   System. (ADR-001)
2. **The Living Graph is never decorative.** It is the project's living intelligence and
   navigation surface. (ADR-002)
3. **Knowledge is never duplicated.** One substrate per layer: Knowledge OS (product),
   Project Memory (per-project), Product Intelligence (product evolution). (ADR-004)
4. **AI assists, humans decide.** Recommendations require human approval; the system never
   acts on irreversible decisions on its own.
5. **Every important object belongs to the graph.** People, resources, risks, issues,
   decisions, documents, meetings, communication, and capacity are nodes, not islands.
6. **Every feature must be explainable by Isabella.** If the AI cannot explain it from
   deterministic rules and real data, it is not done. (ADR-005)
7. **Intelligence before automation.** Understand and explain first; automate only what is
   already understood.
8. **Relationships before lists.** The product's value is in how things connect, not in flat
   tables.
9. **Truth is deterministic.** Status, health, risk, and dependency are computed from rules and
   evidence — never guessed, never conflated. (ADR-006)
10. **Nothing important is ever silently lost.** Decisions, capabilities, and regressions are
    recorded. The product remembers. (ADR-007)
11. **Product Intelligence™ is the source of truth.** It overrides conversation history,
    prompts, and temporary AI context. (ADR-000)

---

> DNA is the constitution's preamble. The ADRs are its laws. The registries are its census.
