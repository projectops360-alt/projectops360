# Organizational Learning Lifecycle

**Plan:** Phase 5 · P5-T1  
**Status:** Implemented

ProjectOps360° promotes project knowledge into organizational learning only when reliable historical
outcomes exist. The lifecycle is:

`Finding → Pattern → Repeated Evidence → Validated Learning → Practice → Retired`

The deterministic evaluator accepts only active Project Knowledge Objects with medium/high confidence,
complete non-synthetic history, passing event integrity, a recorded outcome, and evidence references.
Repeated Evidence requires at least three distinct cases across two projects, a support ratio of at
least two-thirds, contradiction no greater than 25%, and evidence no older than 180 days.

Finding, Pattern, and Repeated Evidence are analytical classifications. Validated Learning and Practice
require separate human owner/admin approvals with rationale. AI/system actors cannot approve. Stale,
contradictory, synthetic, incomplete, cross-organization, or outcome-free histories never become a
validated learning or practice.
