How it works

Five steps from project idea to controlled execution.

A simple, guided flow helps teams avoid the usual project confusion: unclear scope, missing owners, disconnected status reports, and decisions that disappear after meetings.

01
Create the projectStart in a personal or company workspace with the right billing and team structure.
02
Define the foundationCapture goals, scope, deliverables, stakeholders, approvals, and governance.
03
Choose the workflowLet AI recommend Agile, Kanban, Waterfall, Scrum-style, or Hybrid delivery.
04
Build the teamAdd internal users, groups, vendors, stakeholders, approvers, and responsibilities.
05
Run with visibilityTrack work, risks, decisions, changes, meetings, reports, and project memory.

Give stakeholders the status they need without forcing every viewer into the project team.

ProjectOps360° separates billing from operational roles. Companies pay for internal active users, while external collaborators and viewers can participate with controlled access.
✓
Company-level teamsCreate reusable groups like Development Team, PMO, Steering Committee, QA, Vendors, or Finance Stakeholders.
✓
Project-level rolesAssign people as Project Manager, Sponsor, Business Owner, Approver, Contributor, Vendor, or Stakeholder per project.
✓
Controlled stakeholder accessExecutives, clients, and external partners can view the right information without becoming full paid seats by default.
✓
RACI-ready executionConnect responsibility, accountability, consultation, and communication to deliverables, milestones, risks, and changes.
